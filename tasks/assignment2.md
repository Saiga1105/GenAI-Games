    <h2>Task 2: Picasso, Is That You?</h2>
    <p><strong>Type:</strong> Poster / Visual Scientific Communication</p>
    
    <h3>Goal</h3>
    <p>Turn one of the Ghent research domains into an A1 research poster.</p>
    
    <h3>Brief</h3>
    <p>Choose or receive one of the four domains: building physics, dynamics, concrete, or geomatics. Use AI to create a poster that introduces the domain in a visually attractive and academically credible way.</p>
    
    <h3>Target Audience</h3>
    <p>The poster should be understandable and appealing to:</p>
    <ul>
        <li>Visitors</li>
        <li>Prospective students</li>
        <li>3rd-year bachelor students</li>
        <li>Technically curious non-specialists</li>
    </ul>
    
    <h3>Required Content</h3>
    <ul>
        <li>Title</li>
        <li>3 main research lines</li>
        <li>Why it matters</li>
        <li>Visual hook</li>
        <li>Short inviting explanation of the domain</li>
    </ul>
    
    <h3>Deliverables</h3>
    <ul>
        <li>A1 poster (digital or printable format)</li>
        <li>Short written note explaining the design choices</li>
        <li>"How we did it" documentation</li>
    </ul>
    
    <h3>Scoring Criteria</h3>
    <ul>
        <li><strong>Clarity:</strong> Is the content clear and organized?</li>
        <li><strong>Structure:</strong> Does the layout guide the reader logically?</li>
        <li><strong>Visual Quality:</strong> Is it visually attractive and professional?</li>
        <li><strong>Insight:</strong> Does it capture the essence of the domain?</li>
        <li><strong>Appeal:</strong> Is it engaging for the target audience?</li>
    </ul>
    
    <h3>What It Teaches</h3>
    <ul>
        <li>Disseminating academic work through visual media</li>
        <li>Visual communication and design principles</li>
        <li>Transforming ideas into visual structure</li>
        <li>Audience-oriented design</li>
    </ul>
    
    <h3>How We Did It — Report Required</h3>
    <p>Your team must report:</p>
    <ul>
        <li>Which tools you used for layout, design, visuals, icons, and text</li>
        <li>How you turned raw ideas into poster sections</li>
        <li>How you decided what to leave out</li>
        <li>What AI produced well versus poorly</li>
    </ul>
