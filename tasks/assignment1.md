    <h3>Goal</h3>
    <p>Create a 5-minute presentation on the research topic of another group.</p>
    
    <h3>Brief</h3>
    <p>Your team receives a topic from one of the other research groups. Use AI to quickly understand the topic, structure the story, and create a short presentation that is clear, interesting, and understandable to researchers outside that domain.</p>
    
    <h3>Rules</h3>
    <ul>
        <li>Maximum 5 slides</li>
        <li>Maximum 5 minutes presentation time</li>
        <li>Must use AI for structure and visuals</li>
        <li>Should be understandable to non-specialists</li>
        <li>Must include at least one slide on why this matters</li>
    </ul>
    
    <h3>Deliverables</h3>
    <ul>
        <li>5-slide presentation</li>
        <li>Short speaking notes or AI-generated speech text</li>
        <li>"How we did it" documentation</li>
    </ul>
    
    <h3>Scoring Criteria</h3>
    <ul>
        <li><strong>Clarity:</strong> Is the explanation easy to follow?</li>
        <li><strong>Structure:</strong> Does the narrative flow logically?</li>
        <li><strong>Visual Quality:</strong> Are slides visually appealing and informative?</li>
        <li><strong>Insight:</strong> Does it capture the essence of the research?</li>
        <li><strong>Audience Adaptation:</strong> Are unfamiliar concepts explained well?</li>
    </ul>
    
    <h3>What It Teaches</h3>
    <ul>
        <li>AI for communication and storytelling</li>
        <li>Slide drafting and visual hierarchy</li>
        <li>Concise storytelling and explaining unfamiliar topics quickly</li>
    </ul>
